📚 每日英语长难句精析
The committee, which had been tasked with reviewing the proposed changes to the company's organizational structure, ultimately decided to recommend a comprehensive overhaul, taking into account not only the immediate financial implications but also the long-term strategic goals that the CEO had repeatedly emphasized during the meetings.
1️⃣ 主语
The committee
句子主语，指代委员会。
2️⃣ 非限制性定语从句
which had been tasked with reviewing the proposed changes to the company's organizational structure
修饰 "The committee"，说明委员会的任务是审查公司组织结构的提议变更。
3️⃣ 谓语和宾语
ultimately decided to recommend a comprehensive overhaul
委员会最终决定推荐进行全面改革。
4️⃣ 伴随状语
taking into account not only the immediate financial implications but also the long-term strategic goals that the CEO had repeatedly emphasized during the meetings
委员会在做出决定时，不仅考虑了即时的财务影响，还考虑了CEO在会议上反复强调的长期战略目标。
